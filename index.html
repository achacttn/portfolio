Made with THREE.js and GLSL.

First ever project was to implement tic tac toe. The approach taken was to allocate a value to each square such that a sum of a row, column, or diagonal would result in a magic number, and show tictactoe's mapping onto another game, Number Scrabble. Ultimately, the extension of this mapping does not extend past d=3.
